Changing the Shape of a Polygon Model

Once you create a polygon, you can change its shape within the ABAVO Crossplot.

  1. Hold down the CTRL key and left-click the small black corner (node) of a polygon to select it.
  2. Continue holding the CTRL key, and drag the corner node to change the shape of the polygon.
  3. Release the SHIFT key and the mouse.
  4. Position the cursor over the crossplot view and click mouse button 3 (MB3) to refresh the plot.
