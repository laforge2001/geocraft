Moving a Polygon Model

Once you create a polygon, you can move it to another location within the ABAVO Crossplot.

  1. Hold down the SHIFT key and left-click the small black corner (node) of a polygon to select it.
  2. Continue holding the SHIFT key, and drag the polygon to a new location within the ABAVO Crossplot view.
  3. Release the SHIFT key and the mouse.
  4. Position the cursor over the crossplot view and click mouse button 3 (MB3) to refresh the plot.
