Displaying the Current Location of the Cursor in the ABAVO Crossplot

Click to toggle the cursor broadcast feature on and off.

When this feature is on, a light-yellow plus sign tracks your mouse movements on-screen. The X, Y, points display at the bottom of the screen. Move the mouse to broadcast the current cursor location. Hover the mouse on an area to see a tooltip with the current intercept and gradient points.
