Changing Line Slope in a Crossplot

Use these instructions to change the slope of a line in the Crossplot window.

  1. Hold down the SHIFT key and left-click the small black box at the end of a line to select it.

    2. Tip: When a line is selected, the small black box changes to a large black box.

  2. Hold down the CTRL key, left-click a small black box at the end of a line, and drag the end of the line to change its slope.
  3. Click mouse button 3 (MB3) to refresh the plot.
