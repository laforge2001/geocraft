Zooming Out From an Object in the ABAVO Crossplot View

You can zoom out from an object in the ABAVO crossplot view to get more perspective on the image.

  1. Click Zoom Out.
  2. Position the cursor over the crossplot view.
  3. Scroll the mouse wheel downward to gain more perspective on the data. Scroll the mouse wheel upward for a more detailed (granular) view of the data.
