Horizon Curvature Attributes

The following horizon-based curvature attributes are available in the Curvature Attributes algorithm:

  • Kg: Gaussian Curvature - the product of the principal (min and max) curvatures
  • Km: Mean Curvature - measure of average curvature
  • Da: Dip Angle - rate of change of dip in the maximum dip direction in radians
  • Az: Azimuth - angle in radians of the maximum dip direction
  • Kmax: Maximum Curvature - largest absolute curvature
  • Kmin: Minimum Curvature - curvature perpendicular to maximum curvature
  • Kpos: Positive Curvature - anticlinal shape
  • Kneg: Negative Curvature - synclinal shape
  • Si: Shape Index - local surface morphology independent of scale
  • Kd: Dip Curvature - curvature in the maximum dip direction
  • Ks: Strike Curvature - curvature at right angles to dip curvature
  • Kc: Contour Curvature - curvature of surface map contours
  • Kn: Curvedness - amount of total curvature independent of shape
