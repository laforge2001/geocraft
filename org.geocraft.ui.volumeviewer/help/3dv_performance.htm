GeoCraft 3D Viewer: Tips for Using the Viewer and Improving Its Performance

The following tips will help you use ABAVO and ensure that the plug-in runs at its peak performance:

Tip Explanation and Further Details
Centering an object in the 3D viewer See Making an Object the Point of Rotation in the GeoCraft 3D Viewer
Do not render the Z slice in the 3D viewer unless you are working with a small volume.

If you click the Z Slice check box when you set rendition parameters, GeoCraft will display a warning message with a prompt. You must dismiss the prompt and verify that you want to render the Z slice of a volume before any data is rendered.

Warning! It could take several hours to render the Z slice of a large volume.
